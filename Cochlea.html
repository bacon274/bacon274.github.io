Cochlea

Visualising the functionality of the ear.

2018 - Group Project with Anna Bernbuam, Benedict Greenburg, Benjamin Pheipher, Shivam Bhatnagar and Lidia Dynes-Martinez

Special thanks to Abbey Road Studios and Reactify Music for their support and input throughout the project.

What is it?

The Cochlea is part of the physiology of the human hearing system that converts mechanical vibration into electrical impulses to be sent to the brain.

This installation creates a visualisation of this auditory phenomenon and demonstrates the effects of hearing loss.

Video

My Responsibility

I worked on the visual programming language Max in order to analyse the waveform.

I used a fourier transform to decompose the sound wave into its constituent frequency bands.

I then encoded a message that contained the intensity of each frequency band. This message was communicated via a serial port and interpreted by Arduino.

I was also responsible for part of physical build of the prototype.
