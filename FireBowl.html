FireBowl

The responsible camping companion.

2014

FireBowl is a flat pack camping solution for protecting the ground while making fires.

It was designed to be off the ground, stable, portable and allow good airflow.

Process

During this project I explored an iterative design process, starting with lo-fi cardboard prototypes through to fabricating a final version in stainless steel.


Images of the design and build process.

Solution

FireBowl when dissasembled and assembled.

FireBowl is a stainless steel off the ground fire container. It is stable, portable and allows good airflow to keep the fire burning.
